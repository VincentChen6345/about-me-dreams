  • High School - early 2010s

    When I was a kid in high school my dream was to become a full-time Youtuber. I was inspired by Asian American Youtubers like NigaHiga and KevJumba.

  • Uni - 2015+

    My dream was to be a pro-singer, the next Bruno Mars. I watched a Chinese TV show called 'The Voice of China' and I was very inspired by their skill and technique.

    Then I learned about voice types and since I'm not a male tenor, I wasn't born with a high pop voice. It's the equivalent of trying to be a pro NBA player when I'm 5'8. That was the end of my singer dream.

  • Enter the workforce- 2020s

    Unfortunately I ended up in an industry that I didn't feel passionate about. All I could dream about was infact becoming a self-taught front-end developer. I learned off Udemy courses in my spare time and built some projects.

    I was committed... and then it was 2023...and then ChatGPT came out...and then there were tech layoffs. Fear and doubt got the better of me and I decided to stop coding in 2023.
